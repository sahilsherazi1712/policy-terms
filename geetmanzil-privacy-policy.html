Privacy Policy for GeetManzil

Last updated: November 29, 2025

GeetManzil is an offline app that provides Christian Geets and Zaboors for personal use. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

GeetManzil does not require you to create an account and does not collect any personal information such as your name, email, phone number, or address. We do not track your activity across other applications or websites.

The app currently does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party data collection tools.

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

GeetManzil works completely offline. All app-related data is stored only on your device:

  • Favorites & Collections: The Geets and Zaboors you mark as favorites or add to collections are stored locally using SQLite.
  • Backups & Restore: Backup files are created only when you choose to do so. These ZIP files are saved only in the location you select.
  • Appearance & Settings: Theme preferences and other in-app settings are stored on your device.

None of this information is sent to any server or shared with any third party.

3. Optional Information

When creating a Collection, you may add a name, description, and a local image. This information is stored solely on your device and is never uploaded or shared externally.

4. Permissions Used

GeetManzil may request limited device permissions to provide core functionality:

  • Storage / Media Access: Used only for saving and restoring backup files, and for loading optional images that you add to collections. Only the IDs of these images are stored in backup files; no other personal or device information is collected.

We do not access any other device information.

5. Children's Privacy

GeetManzil is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13, or any personal data at all from any user.

6. Future Features

Future updates may introduce features such as worship time reminders, content categories, or advertisements. If advertisements or third-party services are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated before such features are enabled to explain what data (if any) could be collected.

Currently, GeetManzil does not use third-party ads or analytics, and does not share any data.
